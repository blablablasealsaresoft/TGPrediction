# 4. User's trade executes with remaining amount
#
# Fee limits:
# - Minimum: 0.001 SOL (even for tiny trades)
# - Maximum: 0.1 SOL (capped for large trades)
#
# Example fees at 0.5%:
# - 0.1 SOL trade ‚Üí 0.001 SOL fee (minimum)
# - 1 SOL trade ‚Üí 0.005 SOL fee  
# - 10 SOL trade ‚Üí 0.05 SOL fee
# - 100 SOL trade ‚Üí 0.1 SOL fee (maximum cap)
