The bot uses the Kelly Criterion to calculate optimal position sizes:

```

Example:
Win Rate: 78%
Avg Win: +25%
Avg Loss: -8%

Kelly = (0.78 Ã— 0.25) - (0.22 Ã— 0.08) / 0.25
Kelly = 0.195 - 0.0176 / 0.25

Bot adjusts based on confidence:
ULTRA (90%+): 100% of Kelly
HIGH (80-89%): 75% of Kelly
MEDIUM (65-79%): 50% of Kelly
```
